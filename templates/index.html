Welcome to GMXMDS Setup and Run Platform

The Platform provides you with automated approaches to:

1. Prepare receptor ligand complexes and generate required topology files for Molecular Dynamics Simulations

2. Prepare Peptides and Proteins and generate required topology files for Molecular Dynamics Simulations

3. Perform a test run or full scale Molecular Dynamics Simulations using generated solvated structures and topology files in Gromacs

Please Note that while the generated structures and topology files may be useful in other systems, they are here generated to be compatible with Gromacs

Please click the "QGuides" tab to get a Quick Guide on using the platform
